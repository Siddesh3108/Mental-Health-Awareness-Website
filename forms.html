Answer the questions based on your feelings over the last few weeks.

1. How well have you been able to focus on your daily tasks?

2. Have you experienced disturbed sleep because of worry?

3. In your everyday activities, have you felt like you are playing a useful role?

4. Have you felt capable of making decisions?

5. How often do you feel stressed or tensed?

6. Have you felt you could not deal with your difficulties?

7. Have you been able to enjoy your daily activities?

8. Have you been able to deal with your problems?

9. Have you been feeling sad & depressed?

10. Have you lost confidence in yourself?

11. Have you been thinking of yourself as a worthless person?

12. Have you been feeling reasonably happy and content?
